# iTrix Meeting & Purpose Guide — Controlled Public

## Purpose
Every iTrix meeting serves the same purpose: confirm the real bottleneck and decide,
honestly, whether a scoped evaluation is warranted. The point is durable capability, not a
persuasive demo.

## Product
A discovery meeting walks through where the product may fit at a functional level, framed
around the pressure the team named. It never promises a quantitative outcome before an
evaluation.

## Solution
The meeting's output is a shared understanding of fit and a concrete next step — usually a
scoped evaluation — with clear owners on both sides.

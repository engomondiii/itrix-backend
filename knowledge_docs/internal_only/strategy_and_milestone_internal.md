# iTrix Strategy & Milestone Plan — Internal Only

## Strategy
The internal strategy view ties each account to a commercial path and a priority, and
records the reasoning. It is the source the Strategy agent's internal analysis aligns to.

## Commercialization
Milestone planning tracks how accounts progress toward licensing: the gates, the owners,
and the internal probability reads. These signals are computed on the team plane and
surfaced only through team-JWT cockpit endpoints.

## Purpose
Even internally, the purpose is unchanged: durable capability confirmed by evidence. The
milestone plan measures progress toward that, not toward a single closed deal.
